Extensions and Addons

Since V7, Pen+ supports addons and sister extensions. All extensions on this gallery are reviewed and archived for future users

Since Pen+ supports Turbowarp, Penguinmod, and Unsandboxed some addons may not support all scratch modifications!

It should go without saying that these extensions do not work on Scratch.
Not everything on here could be by the Pen Group. Go check out the contributors
